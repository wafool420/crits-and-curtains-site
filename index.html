It's Showtime: Crits & Curtains

The show must go on!

It’s Showtime! A once-beloved theatre troupe is on the brink of bankruptcy, and their only hope lies in delivering the performance of a lifetime! But this isn’t just any show—
it’s their most ambitious, high-stakes production yet:
Crits & Curtains!

The script? A grand fantasy epic where the evil Goddess of Nothing, Dawn, has seized control of the world of Stageoria, with the stage itself being a battlefield where
every scene is an all-out war of spectacle. The audience demands an unforgettable performance—and failure is not an option.b>

BUT UH OH!! There’s just one problem… you had zero time to plan or rehearse! The story is a mess, the set pieces are barely standing, and, oh yeah, you don’t even know
what enemies you’ll be fighting! But in true theatre fashion, you and your troupe have made one fateful decision:

The show must go on.

With no script to guide you, you’ll have to improvise your way through a chaotic, ever-changing adventure—facing unpredictable foes, weaving together random plot points,
and executing your roles flawlessly. Every move is a performance, every mistake is a potential disaster, and only perfect teamwork will save the show… and your troupe’s
future.

With hundreds of people watching, the pressure's on. Will this be the breathtaking revival that cements your legend? ... Or will the final curtain fall on It’s Showtime
forever?
